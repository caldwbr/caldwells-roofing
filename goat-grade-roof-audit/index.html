FAQ

How is this different from the $300 Superior Audit?

G.O.A.T. includes everything in Superior + drone imagery and expanded documentation. It’s built for roofs that need iron-clad documentation for owners, buyers/sellers, HOAs, or adjusters.

Do you fly the drone everywhere?

Where local regulations and safety allow. If airspace or weather prevents a flight, we’ll capture equivalent detail from the ground and note that in the report.

Can you do this for flat roofs?

Yes. For large commercial membranes, see our Commercial Flat Roof Inspection page (PVC/TPO/EPDM, $0.25/sf, $150 min). G.O.A.T. upgrades can be added to commercial scopes too.
