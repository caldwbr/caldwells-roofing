FAQ

How do you price the inspection?

~$0.25 per square foot with a $150 minimum. Large sites or multi-building campuses can be quoted as a day-rate if that’s more economical.

Do you provide repairs if issues are found?

Yes. We can perform minor maintenance items on the spot (if authorized) or provide a detailed repair proposal with photos and line items.

Can you coordinate with building management/tenants?

Absolutely. We’ll work with your site contact for roof access and schedule to minimize disruption.

What about infrared or core cuts?

Those are optional add-ons for moisture mapping or assembly identification. If warranted, we’ll recommend them and price separately.
