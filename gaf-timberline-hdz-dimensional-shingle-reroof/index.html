GAF Timberline HDZ Dimensional Shingle Roofing

Dimensional (architectural) shingles are the most commonly used type today in the South.

Our typical install includes GAF synthetic felt, GAF ProStart starter strip (helps glue down eave and rake edge against wind uplift), architectural body shingles, new boots (LifetimeTool.com where budget allows), drip edge (if missing/deteriorated), new flashings as required, GAF Cobra3 ridge vents, and hip/ridge shingles (GAF TimberTEX caps recommended). We also install ice and water shield and metal in valleys under the shingles.
