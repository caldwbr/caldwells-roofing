About Caldwell’s Roofing

Caldwell’s Roofing is an Auburn–Opelika roofing contractor focused on clean geometry, watertight detailing, and long-life systems. We install standing seam & classic rib metal, architectural/designer shingles, and PVC/TPO membranes with tapered insulation, custom edge metals, and crisp terminations.

Owner: Brad Caldwell • Alabama License: #24152

What We’re Known For

  • Detail-obsessed installs: straight courses, proper valleys/sidewalls/chimney flashings, sealed fasteners.
  • Low-slope done right: tapered insulation for drainage, heat-welded seams, clean scuppers & drains.
  • Metal expertise: true standing seam (mechanical/snap-lock), classic rib, and curved metal applications.
  • Clear scopes & photos: line-item estimates with before/after documentation.

Service Area

Regular work across Auburn, Opelika, Grand National, Oak Bowery, Beauregard, Waverly, Loachapoka, plus Lee, Russell, Macon, and Tallapoosa Counties.

Systems & Materials

  • Shingles: architectural & designer profiles with proper underlayments and ventilation.
  • Metal: standing seam (mechanically seamed and snap-lock), classic rib/ag panels, custom edge metals.
  • Flat roofs: PVC/TPO membranes, tapered insulation plans, term bars, edge terminations, scuppers/outlets.
  • Accessories: skylights, pipe boots, crickets, wall flashings, and transitions.
