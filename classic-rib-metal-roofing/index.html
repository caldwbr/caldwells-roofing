Classic Rib AG Profile Metal Roofing

Classic-rib and snap-lok standing seam are two very popular profiles of metal roofing; however, there are more than a dozen profiles available, seven of which have been illustrated in the image below.

In the banner image above, we used R-panels, which look about like classic-rib, except they have straighter edges as opposed to more rounded edges, on the profile (compare profiles below).

Classic-rib or ag-profile metal can look great on certain applications; beware the hundreds of screw penetrations with neoprene-washers, they may need changing (with thicker screws) in 10 years.
