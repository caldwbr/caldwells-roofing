Starter Strip on Perimeter of Roofing

Every quality roofing system starts with a strong foundation. At Caldwell's Roofing, we understand that the first course of shingles and the perimeter of your roof are its most vulnerable points. That's why we use high-quality starter strips, like the **GAF ProStart® Starter Strip**, to ensure your roof is built to last.

Benefits of a Quality Starter Strip

A properly installed starter strip is not an optional extra—it's a critical component that provides crucial protection and a finished look for your entire roofing system. Here are the key benefits:

1. Unbeatable Wind Uplift Protection

The edges and corners of your roof are where wind can cause the most damage, leading to lifted or torn-off shingles. The GAF ProStart® starter strip features a strong adhesive sealant that securely glues down the very first row of shingles, creating a continuous bond. This drastically improves your roof's wind resistance, especially during severe Alabama storms.

2. Prevents Shingle Sagging for a Clean Finish

Without a proper starter strip, the first course of shingles can curl or droop over the drip edge, which not only looks unprofessional but also compromises the roof's integrity. The GAF ProStart® strip provides a straight, solid line for the shingles to be laid against, ensuring a clean, uniform perimeter that protects your roof and enhances its curb appeal.

3. Ensures Full Warranty Coverage

GAF, a leading manufacturer, requires the use of their starter strips as part of the GAF Lifetime Roofing System to qualify for enhanced warranties. By using a compatible starter strip, you can ensure your entire roof system is protected under a long-term manufacturer warranty.

4. Saves Time and Labor

Pre-cut starter strips are designed to fit the perimeter perfectly, saving our installers valuable time. Unlike cutting shingles on-site, using a dedicated starter strip is more efficient and provides a better, more consistent result.

Starter Strip FAQ

What is the difference between a starter strip and a regular shingle?

A starter strip is a specially designed component with a factory-applied adhesive strip. It provides a clean, sealed edge and ensures the first row of shingles has a solid base. Cutting up regular shingles for the first row is not recommended as it lacks the adhesive sealant needed for proper wind protection.

Is a starter strip really required?

Yes. A starter strip is a required component of a proper roofing system. Without it, your roof is more vulnerable to wind damage, and you may void the manufacturer’s warranty on the shingles.

Does using a starter strip affect the cost of my new roof?

A starter strip is a standard, low-cost component included in every professional roof replacement quote. It adds very little to the overall price but provides significant long-term protection and value.
