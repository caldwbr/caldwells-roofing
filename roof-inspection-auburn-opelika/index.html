Why Inspect?

  1. After an installation (new roof or reroof): cheap insurance you got what you paid for
  2. After a hail or wind storm: make sure there are no insidious issues about to cause costly leak damage
  3. After 5-10 years: ensure there are no pending or long-standing issues causing fungal damage to your decking or home

Caldwell's Checklist: What We Look For:

Installation & Craftsmanship

  • Starter course, proper overhang, and straight course lines
  • Sidewall (or step flashing) & counter-flashing at walls; kick-out flashings present where needed
  • Chimney saddles/crickets, apron/pan flashings, and reglet work

Storm, Hail, & Wind Damage

  • Hail strikes: bruising, mat fracture, spalling, and loss of coating
  • Wind lift: creased shingles, broken seals, displaced caps, missing tabs
  • Impact to metals: gutters, vents, drip edge, ridge caps, soft metals

Aging & Moisture Red Flags

  • Degranulation patterns, brittle or curled shingles
  • Ongoing slow leaks: staining, deck deflection, punky sheathing
  • Attic checks (when accessible): darkened nail tips, fungal growth, insulation moisture, daylight where it shouldn’t be

Flashings, Penetrations, & Edges

  • Pipe boots: neoprene cracking, dry rot, split collars
  • Skylights: curb flashing, saddle, and manufacturer-specific details
  • Drip edge & rake metals: placement under/over felt, gapping, sealant misuse
  • Gable returns/dutch hips where water can track behind fascia

Low-Slope & Transition Trouble Spots

  • Improper shingles on low-slope or flat areas (dead valleys, porches)
  • Ponding, poor taper, clogged scuppers & drains
  • Membrane weld quality (PVC/TPO), probe-tested laps, term bars, edge securement
  • Shingle-to-membrane transitions with correct back-water laps

Ventilation & Heat Management

  • Balanced intake/exhaust (soffit + ridge) vs. “only ridge” or “only box vents”
  • Competing systems (ridge + power fan) that short-circuit airflow
  • Bathroom/kitchen dryer vents dumped into the attic (condensation risk)

  • Insurance-friendly documentation when storm damage is found
