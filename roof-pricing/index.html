Hail Damage Assessment Free

For insurance claims related to hail, wind, or storm damage, the cost of your roof is often covered by your policy, minus your deductible. It's important to understand the difference between **Actual-Cash Value (ACV)** and **Replacement Cost Value (RCV)** policies, as ACV policies depreciate the value of your aged roof, while RCV policies will cover the full cost once the project is complete. We can help you navigate this process.

Why These Prices?

Our pricing reflects the **quality of materials, expert craftsmanship, and the complexity** of the job. For example, installing a standard 3-tab shingle roof is less labor-intensive than a standing seam metal roof, which requires custom fabrication, precise hand-seaming, and extensive flashing work. The wide price range for specialty materials like copper and real slate reflects the significant difference in labor and material costs associated with these premium options.

We stand behind our work and are committed to providing a roof that will protect your home for years to come.
