Popular Shingle Lines We’re Asked About

This page helps homeowners compare color families across common product lines in the Auburn–Opelika market.

GAF

  • Timberline HDZ® (architectural/dimensional; the best-selling shingle in North America)
  • Royal Sovereign (3-tab; long-standing economy line)
  • Note: GAF also produces premium “designer” lines (Grand Canyon, Slateline, etc.), but most homeowners compare Timberline HDZ and Royal Sovereign first when shopping Opelika-Auburn roofing.

IKO

  • Cambridge (architectural/dimensional)
  • Dynasty (architectural; larger nailing zone)
  • Marathon Plus AR (3-tab)

Atlas

  • Pinnacle Pristine (architectural; Scotchgard algae protection)
  • StormMaster Shake / StormMaster Slate (polymer-modified architectural)
  • Note: We haven’t found a current Atlas 3-tab line. Past names included “GlassMaster” / “ToughGlass,” but they appear to be discontinued in many markets.

Owens Corning

  • Duration family (architectural)
  • Supreme (3-tab)

CertainTeed

  • Landmark (architectural)
  • XT25 / CT20 (3-tab)

Tamko

  • Heritage (architectural)
  • Elite Glass-Seal (3-tab)

Malarkey

  • Vista / Highlander AR (architectural)
  • Legacy (architectural; SBS-modified)
  • Windsor (premium thick-profile—similar “high-relief” aesthetic to GAF Grand Canyon-style designer looks)

If you’re trying to match a specific line or color (e.g., “Cambridge Dual Black,” “Dynasty Glacier,” “Landmark Weathered Wood”), call us and we’ll bring real samples so you can verify tones on your roof, in your light.
