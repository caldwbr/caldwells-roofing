EPDM Rubber Flat Roofing

EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer) is a synthetic rubber membrane renowned for its durability, elasticity, and excellent resistance to weathering, UV rays, and extreme temperatures. It's a cost-effective, long-lasting solution for low-slope and flat roofs on everything from residential additions to commercial buildings.

Our installation process focuses on creating a seamless, watertight barrier. We use premium materials like Mule-Hide bonding adhesive for full-adhered systems, ensuring the membrane stays securely in place without seams that could potentially leak. Combined with proper detailing at edges, penetrations, and transitions, an EPDM roof from Caldwell's provides reliable protection for decades.

Why Choose EPDM?

  • Proven Durability: Withstands hail, thermal cycling, and sun exposure better than many other materials.
  • Superior Flexibility: Remains pliable in cold weather and contracts/expands with the roof deck without cracking.
  • Cost-Effective: Offers an excellent balance of performance and lifecycle cost, often with longer warranties.
  • Energy Efficiency: White EPDM reflects sunlight, reducing cooling costs. Black EPDM absorbs heat in cooler climates.
  • Low Maintenance: Resists mold, mildew, and punctures, requiring minimal upkeep over its lifespan.
